# Product Overview

## Project: 2026 웅진마켓 패밀리데이 (Yongjin Market Family Day)

A responsive event promotion website for Yongjin Market's Family Day celebration during Korean New Year 2026 (February 3-5).

### Purpose
Showcase three promotional benefits to customers through a visually appealing, festive web page with traditional Korean New Year aesthetics.

### Key Features
- **Event Header**: Prominent display of event title, dates, and 3D balloon-style branding
- **Benefit Cards**: Three horizontally-arranged cards showcasing:
  - Yongjin Market Member: 2,000 won instant credit
  - Purchase Amount: 10% cashback
  - BC Card: 10% discount
- **Traditional Theme**: Decorative elements including plum blossoms, birds, and kites
- **Responsive Design**: Adapts from mobile (vertical stack) to desktop (horizontal layout)
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant with semantic HTML and keyboard navigation

### Target Audience
Yongjin Market customers accessing the event page on mobile, tablet, or desktop devices.
